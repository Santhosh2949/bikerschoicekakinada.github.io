

# Bikers Choice Kakinada — Premium Bike Modification Website

A dark-themed, mobile-first marketing website designed to grow your Instagram following and drive WhatsApp inquiries. No pricing anywhere — all roads lead to direct contact.

---

## Design System

- **Dark aggressive theme**: Deep black (#0D0D0D) background with steel grey (#111418) cards
- **Neon accents**: Cyan (#00E0FF) for primary actions, Red (#FF0033) for emphasis
- **Bold white typography** with large mobile-friendly touch targets
- **Smooth animations**: Fade-ins, swipe carousels, neon glow effects on buttons

---

## Pages & Features

### 1. Home Page
- **Hero Section**: Full-width dark hero with bold title "Premium Bike Modification & Custom Builds in Kakinada", subtitle about services, and two big CTAs — WhatsApp chat (primary cyan) and Call Now (secondary). Instagram follower badge linking to your profile.
- **Highlights Gallery**: "Our Signature Custom Work" — swipe-friendly image carousel/grid showcasing LED mods, exhaust work, custom paint, wraps, touring setups, and before/after transformations. Minimal text labels per image.
- **Social Growth Section**: "Follow Our Build Journey" — large mobile-friendly buttons for Instagram (primary), Facebook (secondary), and WhatsApp contact. Follower count highlight (4,800+ riders).

### 2. Services Page
- Clean card-based layout organized by category: Customization, Lighting, Performance, Touring
- Each service has a short description with an icon — no prices
- Every category ends with a WhatsApp CTA button

### 3. Gallery Page
- Filterable image grid with categories: Before & After, Custom Builds, LED & Neon Mods, Exhaust Work, Wraps & Paint, Touring Setups, Workshop Shots
- Mobile-optimized with swipe support and lightbox view
- Placeholder images initially (ready for your real photos to be uploaded)

### 4. About Page
- Concise studio story with 4+ years experience and 4.8k follower community highlight
- Bullet-point highlights: Premium finishing, unique builds, LED & exhaust expertise, clean workshop, fast delivery, friendly communication

### 5. Contact Page
- WhatsApp as primary contact (big CTA button)
- Phone number, email, Instagram & Facebook links
- Embedded Google Maps showing your Kakinada location
- Working hours: Mon–Sat 9 AM – 8 PM, Sunday Closed

### 6. Navigation & Footer
- **Sticky mobile navigation** with hamburger menu, neon-accented active states
- **Footer**: Logo, contact details, social links, "Get Directions" link, copyright © 2026

### 7. SEO & Meta
- Optimized title, description, and keywords in HTML head
- Motorcycle Customization Shop schema markup

---

## Key Design Principles
- **Mobile-first**: Large touch targets, swipe gestures, big paddings
- **No pricing anywhere** — all CTAs redirect to WhatsApp
- **Instagram-forward**: Follower counts and Instagram links prominently featured
- **Fast & clean**: Minimal dependencies, smooth scroll animations, neon glow effects
